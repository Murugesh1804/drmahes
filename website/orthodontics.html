What Is Orthodontic Treatment?

Correct Your Bite, Transform Your Smile

Orthodontics involves using braces, wires and other appliances to gently move misaligned teeth and jaws into their correct positions. Treatment corrects crowded teeth, gaps, overbites, underbites, crossbites and more — improving both appearance and oral health.

Properly aligned teeth are easier to clean, reducing the risk of cavities and gum disease. Many patients also experience improved speech, better chewing function and increased self-confidence after orthodontic treatment.

Types we offer: Traditional metal braces, tooth-coloured ceramic braces, and self-ligating braces — Dr. Maheswari will recommend the best option for your case, age and lifestyle.
Benefits

Why Orthodontic Treatment Is Worth It

Straighter, Confident Smile
The most visible benefit — a well-aligned smile boosts confidence in social and professional settings, at every age.
Easier to Clean
Straight teeth have fewer overlapping surfaces where plaque hides. Brushing and flossing becomes more effective, reducing cavity risk.
Improved Bite Function
Correcting bite issues prevents uneven wear on teeth, reduces jaw strain and can alleviate headaches caused by misaligned jaws (TMJ issues).
Discreet Options
Ceramic braces closely match tooth colour and are barely visible. Choose the aesthetic that suits your lifestyle.
All Ages Welcome
Orthodontic treatment is not just for teenagers. Adults achieve excellent results with braces — it's never too late for a straight smile.
Long-Lasting Results
With retainers after treatment, your teeth remain in their new position permanently — a lifelong benefit from a fixed investment.
The Journey

Your Orthodontic Treatment Timeline

1
Orthodontic Consultation & Records
Dr. Maheswari examines your teeth, takes photographs, moulds and X-rays to create a precise treatment plan. The type of braces and estimated duration are discussed.
2
Braces Fitting
Brackets are bonded to your teeth and connected with an archwire. The appointment takes about 1–2 hours. You may experience mild pressure and soreness for a few days, which settles quickly.
3
Regular Adjustment Visits (every 4–6 weeks)
Periodic appointments to adjust wire tension and monitor progress. Each adjustment gently moves teeth closer to their ideal position.
4
Braces Removal & Retainers
Once alignment is achieved, braces are removed. Custom retainers are provided to maintain your new smile — wearing retainers as directed ensures results last a lifetime.
FAQs

Orthodontic Questions Answered

The ideal age for children is 10–14 years when the jaw is still developing, but adults can also achieve excellent results at any age. An early consultation (around age 7–8) allows Dr. Maheswari to monitor development and plan treatment at the right time.

Treatment duration depends on the severity of misalignment. Most cases take 12–24 months. Simple crowding may be resolved in 9–12 months, while complex bite corrections can take up to 2–3 years. Dr. Maheswari will give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.

Metal braces are the most durable and cost-effective option. Ceramic braces are more discreet as they match the tooth colour, making them popular with adults and older teens who prefer a less visible option. Both are equally effective. Self-ligating braces use a special clip instead of elastic ties, which can reduce friction and may shorten treatment time slightly.

You may feel mild pressure and soreness for 2–5 days after fitting and after each adjustment. This is a normal sign that the braces are working. Over-the-counter pain relief (like paracetamol) is usually sufficient. Most patients adjust quickly and find the discomfort manageable.
