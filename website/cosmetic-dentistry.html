What Is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Your Smile, Redesigned

Cosmetic dentistry focuses on improving the appearance of your teeth, gums and overall smile. Unlike purely functional treatments, cosmetic procedures are chosen based on aesthetic goals — whiter teeth, corrected chips, closed gaps, or a complete smile redesign.

Dr. Maheswari takes a personalised approach — assessing your facial structure, skin tone and smile proportions to create results that look naturally beautiful, not artificial. Whether you want subtle improvements or a dramatic transformation, we have a solution for you.

Popular treatments include: Porcelain veneers, composite bonding, professional teeth whitening, gum contouring, and complete smile makeovers combining multiple procedures.
Treatments We Offer

Cosmetic Options at Dr. Mahe's Dentistry

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of teeth to change colour, shape, size or length. Durable, stain-resistant and completely natural-looking.

Composite Bonding

Tooth-coloured resin applied and sculpted directly on the tooth to repair chips, cracks or gaps. A quick, affordable way to improve your smile in a single visit.

Professional Teeth Whitening

In-clinic whitening treatments that safely lighten teeth by several shades in just one session — far more effective than over-the-counter kits.

Smile Makeover

A comprehensive plan combining multiple cosmetic treatments to completely redesign your smile — tailored to your unique facial aesthetics and goals.

Gum Contouring

Reshape uneven or "gummy" gum lines for a more balanced, symmetrical smile. A minor procedure with a significant visual impact.

Smile Design Consultation

A dedicated consultation where Dr. Maheswari analyses your smile, discusses your concerns and presents a digital or visual preview of possible results.
The Process

Your Smile Makeover Journey

1

Smile Assessment & Goals Discussion

Dr. Maheswari listens to your cosmetic concerns, examines your teeth, and discusses realistic outcomes. Photographs and shade comparisons are taken.
2

Treatment Planning

A detailed, personalised plan is created. This may involve a single procedure (e.g., whitening) or a combination of treatments (veneers + bonding + contouring).
3

Preparation & Procedure

Depending on the treatment, minimal tooth preparation may be required. Veneers involve removing a thin layer of enamel; bonding and whitening require no tooth alteration.
4

Final Fitting & Review

Veneers and restorations are tried in, adjusted for fit and colour, then permanently bonded. You'll leave with your transformed smile on the same day in most cases.
