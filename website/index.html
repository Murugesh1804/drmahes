Common Dental Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about appointments, sterilization procedures, and clinic services.

Ensure you have a light meal before scaling or general treatments, unless local/general anesthesia is planned. Bring details of any ongoing medications or systemic health history (like diabetes or hypertension) so our team can plan treatments safely.

When you select a treatment and submit details on our booking wizard, the request is immediately logged inside our local reception desk dashboard. We will review the doctor availability and contact you via phone or WhatsApp to lock in the exact appointment time.

At Dr. Mahe's Dentistry, client safety is non-negotiable. We follow strict international Class-B autoclave guidelines. All instruments are vacuum-packed, sterilized, and opened fresh in front of the patient. Disposable barriers are changed after every single treatment.

We accept all major payments including Cash, Debit/Credit Cards, and UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm). Detailed digital receipts are generated instantly and printed for your insurance record.
