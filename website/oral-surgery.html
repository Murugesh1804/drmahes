Safe, Precise & Comfortable

Oral surgery covers a range of procedures involving the teeth, jaw and surrounding tissues. Dr. Maheswari uses advanced surgical techniques to minimise tissue trauma, reduce post-operative discomfort and speed up recovery. Most patients are back to normal activities within 2–3 days.

What to Expect

The Extraction Process

1

Consultation & X-Ray Assessment

Dr. Maheswari examines the tooth, reviews your X-rays and medical history, and explains the procedure clearly. For wisdom teeth, the position and angulation of the tooth determines the complexity of removal.
2

Local Anaesthesia

A local anaesthetic is administered to completely numb the tooth, surrounding gum and bone. You may feel slight pressure during the procedure but will experience no pain.
3

Extraction

For simple extractions, the tooth is gently loosened and removed. For surgical cases (impacted wisdom teeth), a small incision is made and the tooth may be sectioned before removal to minimise bone removal.
4

Aftercare & Recovery

Gauze is placed to control bleeding. You receive detailed aftercare instructions, antibiotics if needed, and a follow-up appointment. Most patients recover fully within 3–7 days.
